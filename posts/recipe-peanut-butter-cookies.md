## Ingredients

| Item | Quantity |
|:-|:-|
| Flour | 2 ½ cups |
| White Sugar | 1 cup |
| Brown Sugar | 1 cup |
| Baking Soda | 1 teaspoon |
| Eggs | 2 |
| Vanilla | 1 teaspoon |
| Peanut Butter | 1 cup |
| Margarine | 1 cup |

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 °F
2. Roll into balls
3. Press with a fork
4. Bake at 350 °F for 8 minutes
